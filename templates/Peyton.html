The first project I made this school year was a Happy Face Adventure game with three different game modes. Classic, Carnevil, and Tight Squeeze. Each gamemode has a unique twist on the basic Happy Face Game.

Another project I made earlier this year in VEXcode was a program that let a robot chase another robot. My team did this by having one robot print out colors on it's screen while driving a pattern and the other robot would follow those colors.
